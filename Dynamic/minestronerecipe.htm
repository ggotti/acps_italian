Minestrone

You will need:

2 onions (finely chopped)
3 carrots (cubed)
3 sticks of celery (finely chopped)
3 potatoes (cubed)
2 zucchini (sliced)
handful of beans ( cut into small pieces)
1/2 cabbage (finely chopped)
1/4 cup of oil
5 cups of water
2 beef stock cubes
2 tins of Italian peeled tomatoes
1 can of cannellini beans
 

Directions:

Add a oil in a large pot.  Fry gently the onions until soft.  Slowly add all the vegetables gently frying each one as you add to the pot.  At the end add the water, beef stock cubes, tin tomatoes. Simmer gently until all vegetables are cooked.  In the last five minutes add the can of cannellini beans. Serve with hot crusty pasta dura bread.
