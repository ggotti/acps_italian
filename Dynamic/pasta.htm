Pasta

Ingredients

2-1/3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 beaten eggs
1/3 cup water
1 teaspoon olive oil or cooking oil
 

Directions

Stir together 2 cups of the flour and the salt in a large mixing bowl. Make a well in the center of the mixture. Combine eggs, water, and oil in a bowl. Add to flour mixture; mix well.

Sprinkle kneading surface with the remaining flour. Turn dough out onto floured surface. Knead until dough is smooth and elastic (8 to 10 minutes total). Cover and let rest for 10 minutes.

Divide dough into fourths. Roll each fourth on a lightly floured surface into a 12x12-inch square about 1/16-inch thick. Let stand 20 minutes. Cut as desired.

After cutting and shaping the pasta, hang it from a pasta drying rack or clothes hanger, or spread it on a wire cooling rack. Let dry overnight or until completely dry. Place in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 3 days. Or, dry the pasta for at least one hour. Seal in a freezer bag or freezer container, and freeze for up to 8 months.
