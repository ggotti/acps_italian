Tiramisu

for Mums and Dads

Ingredients
4 eggs
4 tablespoons of sugar
2 tablespoons of Marsala
500 grams of Mascarpone
1 packet of Savoiardi biscuits
6 small cups of espresso coffee
a little milk
cocoa for sprinkling


Directions

Separate the yolks from the whites of the eggs.  Beat the whites until soft peaks form.  Mix the yolks with the sugar and Marsala.  Mix the egg whites with the mascarpone.  When that is mixed well add the yolks.  After mixing well prepare the dish in the following way.  Dip the biscuits one by one in the coffee to which has been added a little milk.  Line the bottom of the dish with the biscuits.  Cover the layer of biscuits with the mascarpone mixture.  Continue these layers with a layer of mascapone and sprinkle with cocoa. Refrigerate and serve the next day.
